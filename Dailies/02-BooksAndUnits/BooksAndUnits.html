Books and Units =============== Prerequisites ------------- Shipping books -------------- (This is problem P2.11 from our textbook.) The following pseudocode describes how a bookstore computes the price of an order from the total price and the number of the books that were ordered. Read the total book price and the number of books from the input. Compute the tax (6% of the total book price). Compute the shipping charge ($2 per book). The price of the order is the sum of the total book price, the tax, and the shipping charge. Print the price of the order. Translate this pseudocode into a C++ program.
